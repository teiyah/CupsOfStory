Submission Rules and Regulations

Submissions are open to all writers worldwide, published and unpublished, who are over 16 years old.
Submitted stories should not have been previously published in any format, online or print, self-published or paid.

Entry format required:

  • All stories must be in English
  • All stories must be nameless
  • All stories must be edited and proofread
  • The total number of words should be listed at the end of the story. The maximum is 800 words.

Submission: All stories must be submitted via the online entry form. Stories are limited to one submission at a time per author. No corrections can be made after submission.

Copyright: All stories submitted must be original, fictional, and entirely the author’s own work, and not infringe upon the copyright or any other rights of any third party and not be defamatory of any living person or corporate body. All authors of stories that are published on our website or Instagram will be credited. The author will retain copyright to their story. 

Publication: Publication will be online via the cupsofstory.com website and via the @cupsofstory Instagram. Submission to Cups of Story is done voluntarily and without monetary incentive. Cups of Story is not obligated to provide financial remuneration for submissions selected for publication or otherwise.

Evaluation: All entries will be evaluated by a team of readers. A short list will be drawn up by this team and they will undertake the final selection of the stories to be published. We will not provide feedback on individual stories. All authors that submit their stories will be informed of our decision by email.

Publicity: By submitting, all writers agree that if their story is among those selected, it may be published on the cupsofstory.com website and via the @cupsofstory Instagram, and their work may be featured elsewhere for publicity purposes.
